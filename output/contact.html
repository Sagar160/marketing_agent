Frequently Asked Questions

What are your shipping times? ▼

We typically ship orders within 2-3 business days. Delivery times vary based on your location, but most orders arrive within 5-7 business days.

Do you offer international shipping? ▼

Yes! We ship to over 50 countries worldwide. International shipping rates and times vary by destination. Contact us for a quote.

What is your return policy? ▼

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all products. If you're not satisfied, simply return the item in its original condition for a full refund.

How can I track my order? ▼

Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can use this to monitor your package's progress in real-time.

Do you offer bulk or wholesale orders? ▼

Yes! We'd love to work with businesses and organizations. Please email us at wholesale@cozyhaven.com for bulk pricing and terms.

Are your products eco-friendly? ▼

We're committed to sustainability. Most of our products are made with eco-friendly materials and recyclable packaging. Check individual product pages for details.
